Season 1 · Episode 01

Still Building at 45

The invitation. Meeting Johnny at 15, becoming a mother at 17, building a life — and the quiet realization at 45 that the building isn't done.

Listen on Spotify Apple Podcasts
Season Opener 17 minutes Hosted by Faith

In this opening episode of Still Building, Faith shares the full arc of how she got here — from meeting Johnny at 15, to becoming a mother at 17, to a career in nursing and healthcare leadership, to standing at 45 asking a question she never expected to be asking. This is the invitation into the season.

This episode is for you if you're:

  • In midlife and feeling the quiet shift of something changing
  • Someone who built a life — and is wondering what comes next
  • A woman who has always shown up for everyone else and is starting to ask what she needs
  • Looking for a calm, honest voice to sit with

In this episode

I used to think that by 45 I would feel finished.
Settled. Certain. Done proving. Done becoming.

Instead, I feel aware.
Aware of how much I've built — and how much of myself is still unfolding.

What this season covers

This season explores nine honest conversations about the parts of midlife that don't get talked about enough.

Grief. Love. Building for protection versus building for joy. The friendships that sustain you. The body that keeps changing. The question of who you are underneath all the roles you carry.

Not from the finish line. From the middle — where most of life actually happens.
